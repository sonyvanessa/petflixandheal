SERVING OUR COMMUNITY FOR OVER 35 YEARS

Since our founding in 1990, the clinic has grown from one veterinarian with a support staff of two to seven veterinarians and over 30 support staff – and we’re still growing! At P&H Veterinary Clinic, we lead with excellence in veterinary medicine rooted in prevention, advanced care, and a passion for lifelong well-being. We strengthen the human-animal bond through every interaction, creating positive experiences that foster trust, understanding, and joy. Our team thrives on education, collaboration, and growth, united by compassion and a shared commitment to making a difference. Together, we honor our legacy and build a future of mutual respect, trust, and care for pets, people, and our community. We appreciate your trust in allowing us to care for your family’s pets!
