VETERINARY CLINIC

We are a small-town neighbourhood clinic that offers all the advantages of modern veterinary technology. At Petflix & Heal, we know your pets aren’t just animals, they’re family. That is why we combine professional veterinary care with a touch of love, patience, and playfulness. Our treatment expertise ranges from animals as small as hamsters, to as large as draft horses. We take pride in delivering personalized, compassionate care to our patients in a comfortable veterinary facility that is locally owned and operated with our dedicated farmhouse service. Whether it’s a routine check-up, grooming, or emergency care, our goal is to make sure every tail wags, every purr resonates, and every visit feels safe and comfortable.
